# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The PapsAI XNet community is committed to fostering an open, inclusive, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone, regardless of age, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, nationality, religion, educational background, professional experience, or any other personal characteristic.

Our goal is to build a diverse community of researchers, clinicians, engineers, students, and software developers working together to advance trustworthy and explainable artificial intelligence for cervical cancer screening.

We believe that respectful collaboration, scientific integrity, and open knowledge sharing are essential to achieving this mission.

---

# Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive community include:

- Being respectful and professional in all interactions.
- Welcoming contributors from diverse backgrounds and experience levels.
- Providing constructive feedback and accepting feedback graciously.
- Supporting open scientific discussion and evidence-based decision-making.
- Acknowledging the contributions of others.
- Sharing knowledge openly to improve reproducibility and transparency.
- Respecting different perspectives and experiences.
- Prioritizing patient welfare and ethical AI development.
- Acting with honesty, integrity, and professionalism.

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks, insults, or abusive language.
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private or confidential information without permission.
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of research contributions.
- Knowingly submitting malicious code or intentionally degrading the quality of the project.
- Sharing patient-identifiable information or violating data privacy regulations.
- Any behavior that undermines an inclusive and respectful research environment.

---

# Research Integrity

As an open scientific project, contributors are expected to uphold the highest standards of research integrity.

Contributors should:

- Report results honestly and accurately.
- Properly cite previous work.
- Respect intellectual property rights.
- Clearly distinguish between validated findings and experimental work.
- Follow applicable ethical guidelines when working with biomedical data.
- Ensure that contributions promote reproducibility and transparency.

---

# Responsible AI

PapsAI XNet is committed to the responsible development of artificial intelligence for healthcare.

Contributors should strive to:

- Develop explainable and trustworthy AI methods.
- Minimize algorithmic bias where possible.
- Consider fairness across different patient populations.
- Promote transparency and reproducibility.
- Support ethical and clinically responsible AI deployment.
- Recognize that research software should not replace qualified medical judgment.

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories
- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- Project documentation
- Research collaborations
- Community forums
- Workshops and project meetings
- Any public or private communication representing the PapsAI XNet project

# Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Depending on the severity of a violation, maintainers may:

- Request corrective action.
- Remove inappropriate content.
- Temporarily suspend participation.
- Permanently remove individuals from the project community.

Decisions will be made fairly, consistently, and with respect for all parties involved.
